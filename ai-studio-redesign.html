Problem

The platform initially utilized a "Low-Code" developer experience that, while faster than traditional coding, still required deep technical knowledge to configure and maintain.

This created a significant friction point for teams trying to scale, as the complexity of the orchestration logic still acted as a barrier for those without specialized engineering backgrounds.

Research

We conducted deep-dive interviews with enterprise developers and technical architects to understand their mental models for "chaining" AI tasks.

Key findings revealed that the biggest pain point wasn't writing the code, but understanding the system's state at any given point in an autonomous loop.

Solution

We transitioned the experience into a truly agentic platform that allows users to build enterprise-grade applications using a simple natural language chat interface.

By empowering the AI to handle the "heavy lifting" of orchestration, we lowered the barrier to entry while maintaining a seamless "escape hatch" to code. Developers can instantly switch between the natural language builder and direct code editing for fine-grained control and custom extensions.
